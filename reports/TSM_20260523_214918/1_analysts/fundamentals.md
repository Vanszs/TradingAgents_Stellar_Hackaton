## 6. GROWTH DRIVERS & CATALYSTS

1. **AI Chip Demand Explosion**: TSM manufactures NVIDIA's H100/B200/GB200 AI GPUs, AMD's MI300 series, and Apple's M-series chips. The AI boom is the primary revenue accelerator.

2. **Advanced Node Leadership**: TSM's **3nm (N3)** is the industry's most advanced node in volume production, with **2nm (N2)** expected in 2025-2026. Competitors (Intel, Samsung) have struggled to catch up.

3. **Pricing Power**: As the dominant player, TSM has raised wafer prices 10-20% in recent years, driving margin expansion.

4. **Global Expansion**: New fabs in Arizona (US), Kumamoto (Japan), and Dresden (Germany) diversify geographic risk and align with customer demand for "local for local" production.

5. **CoWoS Advanced Packaging**: TSM's Chip-on-Wafer-on-Substrate (CoWoS) technology is critical for AI chip packaging, with capacity being doubled/ tripled to meet demand.
